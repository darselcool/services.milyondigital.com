Brand Evolution

We partnered with our client to thoughtfully develop her brand from the ground up, beginning with a series of brand boards that helped define her visual personality and tone. By closely collaborating and aligning with her vision, we suggested a curated color palette and imagery that truly reflected her style and goals.

From there, we created a detailed brand guidelines document, including a custom logo grid and logo variations to ensure flexibility and consistency across all platforms. To bring the brand to life, we also designed social media posts and engaging reels, helping her content feel cohesive, polished, and uniquely her.

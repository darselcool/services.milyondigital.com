Toque Futsal

Branding, Web Design & Social Media design

Project Overview

We partnered with Toque Futsal to develop a brand identity that truly reflected their status as one of the most successful futsal teams in the USA. They wanted a design approach centered on the player and action - something sophisticated and sober that would let their team colors shine while incorporating an urban edge without losing that refined aesthetic they were after.

Understanding their vision for a player-focused identity, we created a visual system that captures the intensity and skill of futsal while maintaining the sophisticated look they desired. The design solution emphasized clean, bold elements that highlight the action and athleticism of the sport, using their signature team colors as the foundation for an urban-inspired palette that feels both street-smart and polished.

The result was a cohesive brand identity that perfectly balances their championship-winning legacy with a fresh, player-centric approach. Every visual element reinforces their commitment to excellence while speaking directly to the futsal community and fans who appreciate both the technical skill and urban culture that defines the sport.

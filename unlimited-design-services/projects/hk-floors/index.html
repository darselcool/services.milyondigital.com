HK Floors

Branding

Project Overview

We partnered with our client, HK Floors, to thoughtfully shape their brand identity from the ground up. We started with a series of brand boards that helped define their visual personality, emphasizing quality, durability, and modern style values that reflect their expertise in flooring solutions.
By working closely with their team and aligning with their vision, we suggested a curated color palette, textures, and imagery that capture their craftsmanship and reliability. We then created a comprehensive brand guidelines document, featuring a custom logo grid and logo variations to ensure consistent application across digital and physical touchpoints. To bring their brand to life, we designed social media posts and short-form videos (reels) that showcase their work in an engaging, polished, and cohesive way.
